Frequently Asked Questions - LuminoLens

1. What is LuminoLens?

LuminoLens is a range of affordable, scientifically verified blue light filtering glasses designed for students, professionals, coders, and designers who spend long hours in front of digital screens.

2. What problem does it solve?

Prolonged screen use often causes digital eye strain, headaches, and disturbed sleep due to exposure to blue light. LuminoLens helps reduce these effects with certified blue light filters while offering guidance on healthy screen habits.

3. How is LuminoLens different from other blue light glasses?

Unlike generic or overpriced blue light glasses, LuminoLens stands out with:

  • Affordable pricing (₹399 to ₹799)
  • Spectrometer-tested lenses with verified certification
  • Free access to a QR-linked microsite with eye care exercises, research articles, and ergonomic study tips

4. Is the product scientifically tested?

Yes. Each pair of LuminoLens glasses is equipped with certified blue light filtering lenses. Test reports from spectrometer analysis are transparently shared with customers via the QR microsite.

5. What comes with the product?

Along with your glasses, you'll receive access to a dedicated microsite featuring:

  • Daily eye care exercises
  • Verified research on blue light effects
  • Product certification and lens test reports

6. Who is it meant for?

LuminoLens is perfect for anyone with high screen time — especially students, coders, gamers, content creators, professionals, and online learners.

7. What makes it comfortable for long use?

The frames are designed to be lightweight, minimalist, and pressure-free — making them suitable for extended daily use.

8. Do I need a prescription to use LuminoLens?

No prescription is needed. LuminoLens glasses are non-powered and specifically designed for blue light protection.

9. What's LuminoLens' future roadmap?

We plan to expand into UV-protection sunglasses, ergonomic study kits, and AR/VR-compatible eye health solutions. We also aim to partner with schools and coaching centers to promote student eye wellness.

10. Where can I buy LuminoLens?

LuminoLens will be available via our official website, partner stores, and select student platforms. Stay tuned for launch announcements!
